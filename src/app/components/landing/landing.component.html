HS ACADEMY

The goal of this academy is to create platform to let any registered member enjoy ultra tech and personalized facilities by instructing the cloud driven self learning machines from her/his personal page through mobile (or wearable CEETECH ring).

LCC (Local Ceekul Center)

will be a 5 storey, 4D printed and foldable centre at a land area of 100 meter x 100 meter surrounded by concentric green area of 1 Km x 1 Km at every 10 Km for the automated monitoring of all the IOT systems within 10 Km x 10 Km area besides automated health check up and emergency care; automated justice delivery online

RCC (Regional Ceekul Center)

will be a 5 storey, 4D printed and foldable center at a land area of 1 Km x 1 Km surrounded by 10 Km x 10 Km green area at every 500 Km on earth. It will be a center for research, project guidance, nature care, tourism and regional planning etc. Large number of students interested to have hands on experience at the nearby industrial unit will live here for 6 months while those dedicated for planned research will live here for 6 months to 4 years. The Vice Chancellor, RCC will guide all the activities within 500 Km x 500 Km area.

Ceekul will be a 50 storey, 4D printed and foldable building at a land area of 200 Km x 200 Km surrounded by 500 Km x 500 Km concentric green zone where almost 1000 crore persons from whole world will live like a KUTUMB and will enjoy every ultra tech and personalized facility like fooding, lodging, clothing, health, education, communication, justice, security, transport etc AS A Service by instructing the cloud driven self learning machines from her/his personal page through wearable Ceetech ring. The whole Ceekul infrastructure will be designed by the latest virtual reality software with haptic feelings. Ceekul Academy will be at the center where the best researchers and visionaries will live for 6 months to 4 years for creating innovative ideas.

Our Mission

The construction of any Ceekul infrastructure will be such that the whole building could be assembled (or opened) at a click of button by matching the software code and all the building resources could be reused for the construction of any industrial unit.

Any registered member will watch the activities of any Ceekul infrastructure and will enjoy the virtual tour with haptic feelings by filling the respective Id no. The Id of Ceekul will start with 1, RCC with 2, LCC with 3, local unit with 4 etc.
